### Game Design Document (Neverlands.ru Clone)

#### Implementation must follow AGENT.md and GUIDE.md in the repo (Rails monolith + Hotwire).

## Game Overview
- **Vision and Objectives**
  - Recreate the classic experience of Neverlands.ru, maintaining authenticity.
  - Foster community engagement and nostalgic gameplay.

- **Target Audience**
  - Fans of traditional medieval MMORPGs.
  - Players seeking social interaction and deep character progression.

- **Genre and Sub-Genre**
  - MMORPG
  - Turn-based, medieval fantasy

## Gameplay Mechanics
- **Player Movement**
  - Tile-based grid movement
  - Turn-based actions per player input

- **Combat System**
  - PvE: Encounters against monsters and NPCs
  - PvP: Player duels, group battles, arena tournaments

- **Character Progression**
  - Experience points and leveling
  - Stat points allocation per level
  - Reputation and faction alignment

- **Classes, Skills, and Abilities**
  - Distinct classes: Warrior, Mage, Hunter, Priest, Thief
  - Skill trees unique to each class
  - Unlockable abilities through quests and progression

- **Items, Inventory, and Equipment**
  - Diverse items: Weapons, armor, potions, scrolls
  - Special artifacts purchasable with real money (premium items)
  - Inventory management and equipment slots

## World Design
- **World Map and Exploration**
  - Regions: Varied environments (forests, mountains, rivers)
  - Cities and castles: Social hubs, quest centers
  - Static world with designated spawn points and landmarks

- **Environment Features**
  - Gatherable resources (herbs, minerals, fishing spots)
  - Hidden quests and rare encounters

## Economy
- **Currency Types**
  - Gold (main currency)
  - Silver (secondary currency)
  - Premium tokens (real-money purchases)

- **Trading Mechanics**
  - Player marketplace and auction system
  - Direct player-to-player trades

- **Crafting and Item Enchantments**
  - Crafting professions: blacksmithing, tailoring, alchemy
  - Item enchantment and enhancement mechanics

## Quests & Story
- **Main Storyline and Side Quests**
  - Engaging narrative driving player engagement
  - Side quests providing rewards and additional lore

- **Quest Generation**
  - Static and dynamic quest system
  - Repeatable daily quests

- **Special Events**
  - Seasonal and holiday-themed events
  - Community-driven activities and competitions

## Social Features
- **Guild System and Player Groups**
  - Formation of guilds for cooperative play
  - Guild leveling, management, and ranks

- **PvP Arenas and Competitions**
  - Structured tournaments with rewards
  - Ranking systems and leaderboards

- **Chat & Messaging Systems**
  - Global, local, guild, and private chat systems
  - Integrated messaging and friend lists

## Crafting
- **Professions**
  - Fishing, Herbalism (potion-making), Hunting, Doctor (healing trauma post-fight)

## Clan System
- **Player Unity and Collaboration**
  - Clans for strategic alliances and social engagement
  - Clan wars and territory control mechanics

## Additional Features to Implement
- Player Housing
- Pets and mounts
- Achievements and titles
- Detailed combat log and statistics tracking
- In-game moderation and reporting system
- Mobile compatibility (responsive Hotwire/Stimulus implementation)

By including these elements, your clone will accurately represent and enhance the experience players enjoyed with Neverlands.ru.
