# 13. Extended Feature Set

## Player Housing
- Instanced housing plots purchasable via gold/premium tokens; tiers for room count and exterior style.
- Decor system with craftable/premium furniture, trophies, storage chests, and utility stations (crafting benches, portals).
- Visit permissions (public, friends, guild) and showcase mode for achievements.

## Pets & Mounts
- Companion pets provide passive buffs or gathering bonuses; leveling via care and mini-quests.
- Mounts speed travel on overworld map; faction-specific variants and rare drops from events.
- Stable management UI for summoning, feeding, cosmetic customization.

## Achievements & Titles
- Account-wide achievement tracking across combat, crafting, exploration, social milestones.
- Titles and cosmetics granted for major achievements; some confer small social perks (priority in party finder).
- Achievement showcase on profiles, housing, and forum/Discord integrations.

## Combat Logs & Analytics
- Detailed battle reports exportable/shareable; filters for damage/healing taken, ability usage.
- Aggregated stats for balancing insights and esports-style coverage.

## Mobile Compatibility
- Responsive layouts for key screens (map, quest log, chat, combat) ensuring playability on phones/tablets.
- Adaptive HUD that collapses panels and uses gesture shortcuts for turn actions.

## In-game Moderation & Reporting UX Enhancements
- Dedicated panels surfacing policy summaries, penalties, and appeal status to players.
- Contextual tooltips reminding users of community guidelines in chat/arena lobbies.

## Future Roadmap Hooks
- API/webhooks for fan-made tools (character planners, market trackers).
- Cross-promo events with community streamers; embed Twitch chat in arenas via optional panel.
