# 2. Social, Economy, and Meta Systems

## Chat & Messaging
- Channels: global, local (map tile/region), guild, clan, party, private whispers.
- Moderation: profanity filters, GM mute/ban commands, player reporting with evidence snapshots.
- Persistent friend lists plus mail-style inbox for offline messages and item deliveries.

## Guilds & Clans
- Guilds: cooperative PvE/PvP groups with ranks, shared bank, guild quests, leveling bonuses.
- Clans: macro-factions controlling territories; clan wars unlock castle sieges and territory buffs.
- Management UI: applications, approvals, role permissions (invite, kick, start events).

## Economy & Trading
- Currencies: gold (primary), silver (secondary), premium tokens (purchased via real money).
- Auction house with bidding/buyout, filters per item type, transaction tax sink.
- Direct player trades, including secure confirmation flow for premium items.
- Crafting materials and resources can be sold on marketplace; supports stackable items.

## Crafting & Professions
- Professions: fishing, herbalism (potions), hunting, doctor (post-fight trauma healing), plus blacksmithing/tailoring/alchemy per GDD economy section.
- Profession progression tied to character skills but surfaced in user profile for recruitment.
- Crafting stations located in cities; timers and resource requirements defined per recipe.

## Achievements, Titles, Housing, Pets
- Account-wide achievement system awarding titles, cosmetics, premium currency grants.
- Housing instances per player: customizable interiors, storage, trophy display.
- Pets/mounts provide travel bonuses, combat assists, or utility (gathering multiplier).

## Events & Community Activities
- Seasonal/holiday events toggled via feature flags; include limited quests, cosmetics, leaderboards.
- Community competitions (fishing derbies, arena tournaments) with scheduled brackets and in-game announcements.
